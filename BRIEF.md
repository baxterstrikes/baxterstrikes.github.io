# lynnb.design — Strategy Brief

*The professional site Lynn sends with job applications. It does what LinkedIn can't: show the work, and show how she thinks. Modernized off the dead Gatsby stack, kept at lynnb.design.*

## Audience — locked: A
Hiring managers / founders at **progressive edtech, family-tech, and design-led product** teams. The site is judged *as a designer's portfolio*, by people who look at design work all day. Childcare-operations employers are explicitly **not** the target.

## Positioning
> **A designer-experimentalist with real childcare & family domain depth — she frames the question, designs the study, builds it, and mines it for what it taught her.**

Childcare is her **edge**, not her job title. Origin hook: the bio major who loved *designing the experiment as much as running it*. **Kiddex** is the keystone proof.

## Voice
*Granola, tech-forward, Berkeley mom.* Confirmed an **asset** for audience A. Smart, earthy, editorial, range with a little edge. Not Ms Rachel, not influencer gloss.
- **Not:** pastel, bubble fonts, "mama," exclamation points, daycare primary colors.
- **Yes:** warm but sharp, dry confidence, a point of view, minimal-but-human. One interesting adult — not a kids' brand.

## What it shows
Case studies told as **experiments**: question → how she designed the investigation → what she built/produced → what she learned (specific, opinionated, warts included).

- **Flagship (deep):** Kiddex · the new **childcare/coworking-space** concept · Deviation Index (systems design)
- **Supporting (lighter):** the three UX case studies (Trash Panda, NextBook, Plant Parenthood) · narration (3 produced titles = range/craft) · AI for Moms (venture in motion)
- **Domain credibility layer:** childcare experience / résumé — supporting context, not the headline
- **About** (designer-experimentalist origin) + a light **Currently**

## Cut
Facility photos · kids'-book list · "The Ladies of New Torrance" · "Unrelenting Adversity" (only if a re-listen holds up).

## This is secretly two projects
1. **Strengthen / create the work** — turn Kiddex into a rigorous case study, build the childcare/coworking concept from scratch, reframe the rest around learnings.
2. **Build the site.**

Don't pretend it's only (2).

## Success criteria
Reads as a real designer's portfolio · thesis legible in <30s · every case study shows genuine thinking + one sharp learning · **updatable in minutes** · Core Web Vitals green · WCAG 2.2 AA.

## Constraints
Maintainability is priority #1 · keep lynnb.design · modernize off Gatsby.
