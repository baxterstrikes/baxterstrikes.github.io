My path to UX


Humans are fascinating. My passion is understanding what makes people do the things they do and act the ways they act. Growing up in a small town in California, I was aware there was a lot of world out there. I just had to be patient until the day I could access it. At first chance, I made out for San Francisco to get my degree in Biology. Science was my first academic love. I excelled in research planning and the execution of experimental research. Much of my time was spent in organizing my thoughts into extensive lab notes and study sheets.
